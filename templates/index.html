Begain your future

Pushpalal Memorial Secondary School is deeply committed to nurturing happy, confident students who embody respect, integrity, and success.Established in 2057, Pushpalal Memorial Secondary School draws inspiration from its affiliation with the International Curriculum Association, a distinguished British association renowned for overseeing international curricula in schools around the globe. At the core of our ethos lies a profound dedication to prioritising the well-being of our students and fostering a vibrant community spirit. Our vision encapsulates “Transforming and enriching lives through learning by developing thinkers, technology professionals and inventors with diverse and creative skills to be responsible citizens in the global community.” This guides every facet of our educational approach, ensuring that each student's journey is marked by fulfilment, ambition, and accomplishment. Join us as we embark on a transformative educational odyssey, where holistic development and academic excellence converge seamlessly.
